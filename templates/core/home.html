Features

Real-Time Gesture-to-Speech Conversion

GloveCom instantly converts hand gestures into readable text and spoken audio, enabling seamless communication.

Angle-Based Multi-Finger Sensing

Five miniature angle/joystick sensors capture precise finger movements for highly accurate gesture detection.

On-Device TinyML Processing (No Internet Required)

All machine learning inference runs locally on the ESP32 microcontroller— ✔ No cloud ✔ No external servers ✔ Faster response ✔ More privacy

Wireless Connectivity

Recognized gestures are sent to a smartphone via Bluetooth or Wi-Fi, where the app displays text and plays speech.

Custom Gesture Training

Users can train and add their own gestures for personalized communication needs.

Portable and Lightweight Design

Compact glove hardware with low power consumption makes it easy to carry and use daily.

Low-Cost and Scalable

Designed with affordable components, making it accessible for students, families, hospitals, and institutions.

Mobile App Support

The companion app shows recognized gestures, plays speech output, and supports user configuration.

Designed for Accessibility

Created specifically for people with speech and hearing impairments, improving independence and everyday communication.

How it Works

  1. It Stores the userdefined hand gesture using your phone
  2. When You make a hand gesture
  3. The Sensors send data to the ESP32 chip
  4. The TinyML model recognizes the gesture
  5. The glove sends the result to your phone
  6. The phone displays the text and speaks it out loud
