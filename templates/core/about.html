About GloveCom

GloveCom began as an academic innovation and grew into a mission-driven assistive technology startup dedicated to breaking communication barriers for individuals with speech and hearing impairments.

What is GloveCom?

GloveCom is a smart wearable glove that translates hand gestures into text and speech in real time. Using angle-based sensors, an ESP32 microcontroller, and TinyML, the device processes gestures offline and sends the recognized output wirelessly to a mobile application.

Our Mission

To create affordable, portable, and highly accurate assistive tools that empower people to communicate naturally, regardless of hearing or speech limitations.

Milestones

  • Developed fully working prototype with TinyML on ESP32
  • Conducted real-world pilot tests in educational and community environments
  • Created customizable gesture dataset for TinyML model training
  • Designed wireless mobile app for gesture-to-speech output
